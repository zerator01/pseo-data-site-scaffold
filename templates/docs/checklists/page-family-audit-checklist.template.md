# Page Family Audit Checklist

Use this checklist when reviewing one page family at a time.

## Sample Set

- 1 strongest page in the family
- 1 representative mid-tier page
- 1 weak or edge-case page

## Checklist

- The page has a clear query or reader intent.
- The page is not just a variable-swapped version of its siblings.
- The first screen explains why this page exists.
- The body adds interpretation, not just raw numbers or cards.
- Source, methodology, or estimate boundaries are visible where needed.
- The page does not make stronger claims than the data supports.
- Internal links point to the next best comparison path.
- The page would still feel useful if it were not top-ranked.
- The page family has a clear indexing rule:
  - fully indexed
  - selectively indexed
  - not in sitemap yet

## Required Notes

Record for each reviewed page:

- what makes it unique
- what still feels templated
- whether it deserves sitemap inclusion
- what family-wide fix would improve the bottom 50%, not just the top 5%
